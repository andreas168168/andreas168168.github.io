Our Mission for a Sustainable Future

At EcoScanner, we're revolutionizing the way consumers and businesses understand the environmental impact of food products through innovative barcode scanning technology.

Our Story

2018: The Beginning

Founded by environmental scientists and tech entrepreneurs, EcoScanner began as a research project at Cambridge University to quantify the carbon footprint of consumer products.

2019: Technology Development

Our team developed the first prototype of the barcode scanner, capable of identifying products and providing basic carbon footprint data.

2020: Database Expansion

We partnered with leading food manufacturers to build a comprehensive database of product emissions, expanding our coverage to over 100,000 products.

2021: Mobile App Launch

The EcoScanner mobile app was released, allowing consumers to scan products while shopping and make more environmentally conscious decisions.

2022: Retail Integration

Major retailers began integrating our technology into their shopping experiences, with in-store kiosks and online product listings.

2023: Global Expansion

EcoScanner expanded internationally, now operating in 15 countries with localized product databases and partnerships.

2024: Advanced AI Integration

Implementation of advanced AI algorithms to provide more accurate emissions data and personalized sustainability recommendations.

2025: Today

Today, EcoScanner is the leading provider of food product emission data, with over 5 million active users and partnerships with hundreds of businesses worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower consumers and businesses with accessible, accurate information about the environmental impact of food products, enabling more sustainable choices.

We believe that transparency in product emissions data is essential for addressing climate change and building a more sustainable food system. By making this information readily available through our barcode scanning technology, we're helping individuals and organizations reduce their carbon footprint.

Our Vision

A world where every food product's environmental impact is transparent, allowing consumers to make informed choices and driving the industry toward more sustainable practices.

We envision a future where emission information is as common on product labels as nutritional facts, and where this transparency creates market incentives for companies to develop more sustainable products and supply chains.

How We're Making a Difference

Shifting Consumer Behavior

Our user surveys show that after using EcoScanner for three months, people make more environmentally friendly food choices 42% more often, resulting in an average reduction of 1.2 tons of CO2 per user annually.

Driving Industry Change

By providing transparency on product emissions, we've created market pressure that has led 76 major food manufacturers to reformulate products or improve their supply chains for lower environmental impact.

Supporting Sustainable Agriculture

Our partnership program has helped connect sustainable farmers with major retailers, increasing market share for environmentally responsible agricultural practices.

Environmental Education

Beyond product scanning, our educational content has reached over 12 million people, raising awareness about the connection between food choices and climate change.
