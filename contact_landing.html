Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to some of our most commonly asked questions.

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line directly.

Do you offer technical support outside of business hours?

Yes, for our Business Solutions clients, we offer 24/7 emergency technical support. Standard support is available during regular business hours.

How can I schedule a demo of your barcode scanner technology?

You can schedule a demo by filling out our contact form and selecting "Business Partnerships" as the subject, or by emailing business@ecoscanner.com directly.

Do you have partner programs for retailers?

Yes, we offer partnership programs for retailers of all sizes. Please contact our business team for more information about our retail integration options.
