How Our Technology Works

Powered by advanced algorithms and the world's largest food emissions database

1

Barcode Scan

When a customer scans a food product barcode, our system instantly recognizes the unique identifier.

2

Data Lookup

The barcode is matched against our database of over 2 million food products with detailed environmental impact data.

3

Impact Calculation

Our proprietary algorithm calculates the carbon footprint, water usage, land use, and other environmental metrics.

4

User-Friendly Display

Results are presented in an easily understandable format with visual comparisons and actionable information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our business solutions

How accurate is the emissions data?
Our emissions data is calculated using a combination of lifecycle assessment methodologies, supply chain information, and third-party verified datasets. We maintain a 92-95% accuracy rate compared to full LCA studies, with continual updates based on the latest research.
How long does implementation take?
Implementation timelines vary based on the integration method chosen. API integrations can be completed in 1-2 weeks, white-label apps in 3-4 weeks, and in-store kiosk deployments in 4-6 weeks. Our team works closely with you to establish a clear project timeline.
What types of food products are supported?
Our database covers over 2 million food products across all major categories including produce, meat, dairy, packaged foods, beverages, and prepared meals. We have particularly strong coverage in North America and Europe, with expanding coverage in Asia and South America.
Can we add our own product data?
Yes! Enterprise and Custom plans include the ability to upload and integrate your own product data and emissions calculations. This is particularly valuable for private label products or items with supply chain information you already possess.
Is the system GDPR compliant?
Yes, our system is fully GDPR compliant. We offer data processing agreements, maintain strict data protection protocols, and provide transparent information on how consumer data is handled. Personal data is encrypted and anonymized.
Do you offer custom reporting features?
All business plans include comprehensive reporting features. Enterprise and Custom plans offer tailored reporting dashboards that can be designed to match your specific KPIs and sustainability metrics. Reports can be automated and scheduled for delivery.
